About Me

Ed was born in Seoul, South Korea. At age 9, he moved to New Jersey with his family to start a new life in America. Upon graduating high school in 2009, he started colleged in the fall of that year at Davidson College. While at Davidson, he worked as a Student Tech Consultant and Resident Advisor. He also volunteered as a tutor at the Ada Jenkins Center and played for the Davidson College Symphony Orchestra. Upon graduating in May 2013, he began working as a Life Insurance Underwriter at Metropolitan Life Insurance Co. After spending about two years in the position, he transitioned into a role as a Product Consultant for MetLife Direct.
